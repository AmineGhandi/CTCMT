Some frequently asked
questions

who helped you with the first edition, and how did you get the funds for it ?

The success of the first edition was a result of strong community support. The community played a crucial role, offering assistance and expertise. Additionally, the funds for the project came from my personal pocket money, demonstrating a personal commitment to the initiative.

How much time did it take to make the first eddition ?

In a four-month adventure, we found the perfect filming location with high-speed internet, created a unique logo and captivating intro/outro sequences, and meticulously set up the studio. It marked a thrilling and transformative chapter in our project's evolution

How much did it cost to make the show ?

a sad whopping 20 000 dhs (i didn't manage the money well enough :P)

How much do you plan to invest in the next show ?

Although the first edition was costly, upcoming editions won't be as expensive. I believe a budget of 9,000 Dhs per episode would be more than enough to make it a reality.

How frequently do you plan to do the show

We are aiming to host the show on a monthly basis, providing a consistent and engaging experience for our audience. This frequency allows us to carefully curate each episode, ensuring high-quality content and a meaningful connection with our viewers. By choosing a monthly schedule, we can dedicate ample time to preparation, guaranteeing that each episode is a well-crafted and enjoyable experience for our audience to look forward to.
